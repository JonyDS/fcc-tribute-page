Stanley Kubrick's timeline of his life and projects:

  • 1928 - Stanley Kubrick is born on the 26th of July in the Bronx in New York City
  • 1946 - Kubrick becomes a staff photographer for Look (until 1951)
  • 1953 - Release of Fear and Desire
  • 1955 - Release of Killer's Kiss
  • 1956 - Release of The Killing
  • 1956 - Stanley Kubrick and James B. Harris found their own film production company
  • 1957 - Release of Paths of Glory
  • 1960 - Release of Spartacus
  • 1968 - Release of 2001: A Space Odyssey
  • 1971 - Release of A Clockwork Orange
  • 1980 - Release of The Shining
  • 1999 - Stanley Kubrick dies on the 7th of March after suffering a heart attack at his home near St. Albans
  • 1999 - Release of Eyes Wide Shut
